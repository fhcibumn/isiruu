How To Watch Bare Knuckle Boxing: A Beginner's Guide

It’s 2023 and bare knuckle boxing is becoming more and more popular. If you’re new to the sport or just curious about it, you may be wondering how to watch it. Fortunately, it’s easier than you might think. Here’s a beginner’s guide to watching bare knuckle boxing.

Watching Live Events

The first and most obvious way to watch bare knuckle boxing is to attend a live event. Bare knuckle boxing events are held all over the world, and many of them are open to the public. To find an event near you, check out the websites of bare knuckle boxing organizations like the World Bare Knuckle Fighting Federation or the Bare Knuckle Boxing Hall of Fame. When you attend a live event, you’ll get to experience the excitement and energy of the sport firsthand. You’ll see the fighters up close and hear the sound of every punch. Plus, you’ll be able to meet other fans and talk to them about the sport.

Watching on TV

If you can’t attend a live event, the next best thing is to watch bare knuckle boxing on TV. Many cable and satellite providers offer pay-per-view options for major bare knuckle boxing events. You can also check out streaming services like DAZN or ESPN+ for live and on-demand bare knuckle boxing content. When you watch on TV, you’ll get the benefit of expert commentary and analysis. You’ll also be able to see every angle of the fight thanks to multiple cameras and replays.

Watching on Social Media

Another way to watch bare knuckle boxing is to follow the sport on social media. Many fighters and organizations post highlights, interviews, and full fights on platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Twitter. When you watch on social media, you’ll be able to see the best moments of the sport without paying a dime. You’ll also be able to connect with other fans and participate in the conversation around the sport.

Watching on DVD or Blu-ray

Finally, if you’re a collector or just want to watch some classic fights, you can purchase bare knuckle boxing DVDs or Blu-rays. Many of the sport’s most iconic fights are available for purchase online or at specialty stores. When you watch on DVD or Blu-ray, you’ll be able to see the fights in the highest possible quality. You’ll also be able to watch at your own pace and go back and re-watch your favorite moments.

Conclusion

Whether you prefer to watch live, on TV, on social media, or on DVD, there are plenty of ways to watch bare knuckle boxing. With this beginner’s guide, you’ll be able to experience the thrill of the sport no matter where you are. So sit back, relax, and enjoy the show!
