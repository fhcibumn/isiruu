Where to Watch BKFC - The Ultimate Guide for 2023

If you're a fan of bare-knuckle fighting, then you're probably eagerly anticipating the next big event. The Bare Knuckle Fighting Championship (BKFC) has quickly become one of the most popular fighting leagues in the world, and with good reason. These intense and brutal fights are not for the faint of heart, but they're definitely worth watching for those who love the sport.

However, with so many different platforms and channels available to watch BKFC fights, it can be tough to figure out exactly where to tune in. That's why we've put together this ultimate guide for 2023, to help you find the best place to watch all the action.

Streaming Services

One of the easiest and most convenient ways to watch BKFC fights is to use a streaming service. There are several different options available, including:

  • ESPN+
  • Fite TV
  • DAZN
  • UFC Fight Pass

Each of these services offers a slightly different viewing experience, but they all give you access to live and on-demand BKFC fights. Some require a subscription fee or pay-per-view purchase, so make sure you check the pricing before you sign up.

Television Networks

If you prefer to watch BKFC fights on your TV, there are several networks that broadcast the events. Some of the most popular options include:

  • ESPN
  • FOX Sports
  • Paramount Network
  • AXS TV

Depending on where you live and your cable or satellite provider, you may have access to these networks as part of your TV package. If not, you may need to add a sports package or upgrade your subscription.

In-Person Events

Of course, nothing beats the excitement of watching a BKFC fight in person. If you're lucky enough to live near a venue that hosts these events, then you should definitely consider attending. Not only will you get to see the fights up close and personal, but you'll also get to experience the energy and atmosphere of the crowd.

Conclusion

As you can see, there are several different ways to watch BKFC fights, whether you prefer to stream them online, watch them on TV, or attend in person. Whichever option you choose, just make sure you're prepared for the intense and thrilling action that's sure to come.
