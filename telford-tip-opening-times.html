Wellington Household Recycling Centre

The Wellington Household Recycling Centre is located on the Hortonwood Industrial Estate and is open from 8:30 am until 6 pm, seven days a week. This tip accepts a wide variety of household waste, including garden waste, electrical items, and hazardous materials. It's worth noting that commercial waste is not accepted at this location, and there may be restrictions on the amount of waste you can dispose of in one visit.

Halesfield Household Recycling Centre

If you live in the Halesfield area, the Halesfield Household Recycling Centre may be more convenient for you. This tip is open from 9 am until 5 pm, seven days a week, and accepts much of the same waste as the Wellington centre. However, it's important to note that this location does not accept asbestos or plasterboard waste, so you'll need to find an alternative disposal method if you have these materials to get rid of.

Shawbirch Household Recycling Centre

For those living in the Shawbirch area, the Shawbirch Household Recycling Centre is open from 8 am until 4 pm, Monday to Saturday. This tip accepts household waste, including garden waste, and also has a reuse shop where you can donate and purchase items in good condition. It's worth noting that this location is closed on Sundays and bank holidays.

Stanton Household Recycling Centre

The Stanton Household Recycling Centre is located on the outskirts of Telford and is open from 9 am until 5 pm, seven days a week. This tip accepts a wide variety of household waste, but it's worth noting that this location does not accept commercial waste or trade waste. If you're unsure about whether your waste is accepted at this location, it's best to check with the centre before making the trip.
