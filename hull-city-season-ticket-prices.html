Hull City Season Ticket Prices

As the new football season approaches, fans of Hull City are eagerly awaiting the announcement of season ticket prices for the upcoming campaign. With the club's promotion to the Premier League last season, there is a buzz of excitement among supporters, but also a concern about the potential cost of attending matches.

Fortunately, the club has announced that there will be no increase in season ticket prices for the 2023/24 season. This means that fans can continue to enjoy watching their team play in the top flight without having to break the bank.

Early Bird Discounts

One of the ways in which fans can save money on their season tickets is by taking advantage of early bird discounts. The club typically offers reduced prices for those who purchase their tickets before a certain deadline, which can represent a significant saving for supporters.

For the 2023/24 season, the early bird deadline is set for the end of June, so fans are encouraged to act quickly if they want to secure their seat at the KCOM Stadium for the best possible price.

Concession Prices

Another important consideration for many fans is the cost of concession tickets. Hull City offers discounted prices for a range of groups, including under-16s, students, and senior citizens.

For the upcoming season, concession prices will be frozen at the same level as last year, meaning that these groups can continue to enjoy watching their team play without having to pay full adult prices.

Matchday Tickets

For fans who are unable to commit to a full season ticket, matchday tickets are also available. These can be purchased in advance or on the day of the game, although prices are typically higher for the latter option.

It is worth noting that matchday prices can vary depending on the opposition and the time of the match, so fans should check the club's website for the latest information before making a purchase.

Season Ticket Benefits

Finally, it is worth highlighting some of the benefits that come with purchasing a season ticket. In addition to securing a seat at every home game, season ticket holders also receive priority access to away tickets, as well as discounts on merchandise and other club-related events.

Furthermore, by purchasing a season ticket, fans are showing their support for the club and helping to create a vibrant atmosphere at the KCOM Stadium. So, for those who can afford it, a season ticket can be a great investment in both their own enjoyment and the success of the team.
