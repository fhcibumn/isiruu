Cheesy Bacon Pierogi Bake - A Delicious Comfort Food

Are you looking for a satisfying and comforting dish to warm up your taste buds? Look no further than the cheesy bacon pierogi bake! This delicious and easy-to-make recipe will leave you feeling full and happy.

Made with soft and pillowy pierogies filled with mashed potatoes and cheese, combined with crispy bacon and melted cheddar, this dish is a perfect blend of savory flavors. It's the ultimate comfort food that will have you coming back for seconds.

Soft and Pillowy Pierogies

The key ingredient in this cheesy bacon pierogi bake is, of course, the pierogies. These delicious dumplings are a traditional Polish dish made with unleavened dough and filled with various ingredients. In this recipe, we use pierogies filled with mashed potatoes and cheese, which provide a soft and pillowy texture that pairs perfectly with the other ingredients.

To cook the pierogies, simply boil them in salted water until they float to the top. Drain them and set them aside while you prepare the other ingredients.

Crispy Bacon

Bacon adds a crispy and salty element to this dish that perfectly complements the cheesy pierogies. Simply cook the bacon in a skillet until it's crispy, then chop it into small pieces.

Melted Cheddar Cheese

No cheesy bacon pierogi bake would be complete without melted cheddar cheese! Simply shred some cheddar cheese and sprinkle it on top of the pierogies and bacon before baking.

Bake and Serve

Once you have all the ingredients ready, it's time to assemble the cheesy bacon pierogi bake. Layer the pierogies in a baking dish, then sprinkle the chopped bacon on top. Finally, cover everything with shredded cheddar cheese.

Bake in a preheated oven at 350°F for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Serve hot and enjoy!
