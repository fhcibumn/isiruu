Girl Scout Cookies 2023 Season

The year 2023 brings another exciting season for Girl Scout Cookies enthusiasts. The highly anticipated annual cookie sale is back, with more flavors and new packaging designs. Girl Scout Cookies have been a beloved treat for many years, and this year's selection promises to be just as delicious as ever.

If you're a fan of Girl Scout Cookies, you'll be happy to know that the 2023 season brings back some of the classic favorites, such as Thin Mints, Samoas, and Tagalongs. But there are also some new flavors that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Let's take a closer look at what's in store for the 2023 Girl Scout Cookies season.

New Flavors

This year, the Girl Scouts are introducing three new flavors: Caramel Chocolate Chip, Lemon-Ups, and Toast-Yay!. Caramel Chocolate Chip is a chewy cookie with semi-sweet chocolate chips and a caramel finish. Lemon-Ups are crisp lemon cookies with inspiring messages on them, such as "I am a go-getter" and "I am an innovator." Toast-Yay! is a toast-shaped cookie with icing and sprinkles on top, reminiscent of a popular breakfast pastry.

These new flavors are a welcome addition to the already impressive lineup of Girl Scout Cookies. They offer a fresh twist on classic flavors and provide a new experience for those who have been enjoying Girl Scout Cookies for years.

Healthy Options

In addition to the new flavors, the Girl Scouts are also offering some healthier options for those who are watching their sugar intake. The Toffee-tastic cookie is gluten-free and made with rich, buttery toffee bits. The Lemonades cookie is also gluten-free and has a tangy lemon icing on top.

For those who are calorie-conscious, the Thin Mints and Trefoils cookies are both under 40 calories per cookie. This makes them a guilt-free treat that you can enjoy without worrying about breaking your diet.

Packaging Redesign

One of the most noticeable changes for the 2023 Girl Scout Cookies season is the packaging redesign. The new packaging features a more modern design with vibrant colors and bold fonts. The Girl Scout logo is prominently displayed, along with pictures of the cookies and flavor descriptions.

The new packaging also includes nutritional information, making it easier for consumers to make informed choices about what they're eating. The packaging is also more environmentally friendly, with a thinner and more recyclable material being used.

How to Get Your Hands on Girl Scout Cookies

If you're ready to indulge in some delicious Girl Scout Cookies, there are a few ways to get them. The traditional way is to find a Girl Scout selling cookies in your area. You can also order cookies online through the Girl Scouts website or through a local troop's cookie website.

Another option is to use the Girl Scout Cookie Finder app. This app allows you to find cookie sales near you, and even lets you order cookies for delivery or pickup. It's a convenient way to get your fix of Girl Scout Cookies without leaving your home.

Overall, the 2023 Girl Scout Cookies season promises to be another exciting time for cookie lovers. With new flavors, healthier options, and a fresh packaging design, there's something for everyone to enjoy.
