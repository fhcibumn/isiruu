What Time Does Tesco Close Today?

It's a common question people ask themselves every day: What time does Tesco close today? Whether you're in a rush to grab some groceries after work or need to make a last-minute purchase, knowing the opening hours of your local Tesco store is essential. Here's everything you need to know about Tesco's opening and closing times.

Weekday Opening Hours

Most Tesco stores open at 7am on weekdays and close at 10pm. However, some stores may have slightly different opening hours, so it's always best to check your local store's hours online or by calling them directly. Some larger Tesco stores may also have extended hours and open earlier or close later.

Weekend Opening Hours

On Saturdays, most Tesco stores open at 7am and close at 10pm, just like on weekdays. However, on Sundays, Tesco stores usually have shorter opening hours, with most stores opening at 10am and closing at 4pm. Again, it's always best to double-check your local Tesco store's hours before heading out.

Bank Holiday Opening Hours

On bank holidays, Tesco stores may have different opening hours than usual. Some stores may open later or close earlier, so it's important to check your local store's hours ahead of time. You can usually find this information on Tesco's website or by calling them directly.

Christmas and New Year Opening Hours

During the festive season, Tesco stores may have shorter opening hours on Christmas Eve, and may be closed on Christmas Day and Boxing Day. On New Year's Eve, stores may close earlier than usual. You can find up-to-date information on Tesco's website or by calling your local store directly.
