Ezetimibe 10 mg Oral Tablet: The Latest Drug to Lower Cholesterol

Ezetimibe is taken as a single tablet once daily, which makes it easy to incorporate into a daily routine. It can be taken with or without food, and it does not require any special monitoring or testing.

How to Take Ezetimibe 10 mg Oral Tablet
