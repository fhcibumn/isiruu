What Channel is the Dodger Game On? - News Blog 2023

It's a beautiful day in Los Angeles and you're excited to watch the Dodgers play. But before you can sit back and enjoy the game, you need to figure out what channel it's on. With so many options and streaming services available, it can be overwhelming to find the right channel. In this article, we'll break down all the ways you can watch the Dodgers play and what channel you can find them on.

Local TV Channels

If you live in the Los Angeles area, you can watch the Dodgers on local TV channels such as KTLA, SportsNet LA, and KCAL. KTLA is a free-to-air channel that broadcasts some Dodgers games throughout the season. SportsNet LA, on the other hand, is a regional sports network that exclusively carries the Dodgers game. If you have a cable subscription, you can also watch the Dodgers on KCAL, which broadcasts a handful of games each season.

Streaming Services

If you don't have cable or a TV, you can still watch the Dodgers on streaming services such as Hulu Live, Sling TV, and YouTube TV. These services offer live TV channels, including SportsNet LA, that you can access through a subscription. Some services also offer free trials, so you can watch a few games for free before committing to a monthly subscription.

MLB.TV

If you're a die-hard Dodgers fan and want to watch every game, you can subscribe to MLB.TV. This service offers live streaming of every out-of-market game, including the Dodgers. However, keep in mind that you won't be able to watch games that are broadcasted on local TV channels, such as KTLA or SportsNet LA.

Radio

If you're on the go or don't have access to a TV, you can listen to the Dodgers game on the radio. You can tune in to AM 570 LA Sports or FM 102.7 KIIS FM to hear the game. You can also stream the game live on the iHeartRadio app.
