When you travel to Thailand, you may hear the phrase "มี ม สวัสดี วัน จันทร์" (mii m sa-wat-dii wan jan) on Mondays. The Meaning of "มี ม สวัสดี วัน จันทร์"

The phrase "มี ม สวัสดี วัน จันทร์" can be translated to "have a good Monday" in English. However, the phrase is more than just a simple greeting. In Thai culture, each day of the week is associated with a specific color and planet. Monday is associated with the moon and the color yellow. The number nine is also considered lucky on Mondays, which is why the phrase includes the letter "m" (which represents the number nine in Thai).

By saying "มี ม สวัสดี วัน จันทร์", Thais believe that they are offering good wishes and positive energy to others on this auspicious day. It is a way to show respect and goodwill towards others, and to acknowledge the importance of astrology and numerology in Thai culture.
