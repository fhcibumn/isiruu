In 2023, Harry Potter fans are buzzing with excitement as the beloved series has finally arrived on Disney Plus. However, some fans are struggling to find the wizarding world on the streaming service. Fear not, as we have created a guide on how to watch Harry Potter on Disney Plus.

1. Check Your Disney Plus Subscription

Before you start searching for Harry Potter on Disney Plus, make sure you have a Disney Plus subscription. If you are yet to sign up, head to the Disney Plus website and create an account. Once you have confirmed that Harry Potter is available in your region, it's time to start searching for the series on Disney Plus. You can use the search bar on the top of the Disney Plus homepage to find the series. Simply type in "Harry Potter" and hit enter.

If you are not sure what to watch first, Disney Plus has made it easier for you. The Harry Potter films are displayed in chronological order, so you can start with the first movie and work your way through the series.
