Welcome to the world of technology where everything is possible. In this fast-paced era, security is one of the top priorities for every individual. Video doorbells have become a popular household item over the years, and for a good reason. They provide an extra layer of security and convenience, allowing you to see and speak to anyone who comes to your door, whether you are at home or not.

As the year 2023 dawns upon us, we have compiled a list of the best video doorbells available in Australia. Whether you are looking for a simple and affordable option or a high-end and feature-rich one, we have got you covered.

Wireless Video Doorbells

Wireless video doorbells have become increasingly popular in recent years due to their ease of installation and convenience. They typically use Wi-Fi to connect to your smartphone or tablet, allowing you to see and communicate with visitors from anywhere in the world.

The Ring Video Doorbell 3 is one of the best wireless options available in Australia. It features a 1080p HD camera with night vision, two-way audio, and customizable motion detection. It also has a removable and rechargeable battery, making it easy to install without any wiring.

The Arlo Video Doorbell is another excellent wireless option. It has a 180-degree viewing angle, 1080p HD camera, and two-way audio. It also has advanced features such as person detection and package detection, which sends you alerts when someone is at your door or when a package is delivered.

Wired Video Doorbells

Wired video doorbells require a bit more installation effort, but they tend to be more reliable and offer better video quality. They connect to your existing doorbell wiring and typically come with a chime or speaker that rings when someone presses the button.

The Nest Hello Video Doorbell is one of the best wired options available in Australia. It features a 160-degree viewing angle, 4:3 aspect ratio, and HDR video. It also has facial recognition, which allows you to know who is at your door even before they ring the bell.

The Hikvision Video Intercom Doorbell is another excellent wired option. It has a 2-megapixel camera with night vision and a 180-degree viewing angle. It also has a built-in microphone and speaker, allowing you to communicate with visitors from your smartphone or tablet.

Smart Home Integration

Smart home integration has become a popular trend in recent years, allowing you to control various devices in your home using your voice or smartphone. Many video doorbells now come with smart home integration, allowing you to connect them to your existing smart home devices.

The August Doorbell Cam Pro is one of the best video doorbells with smart home integration. It works with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit, allowing you to control it using your voice or smartphone. It also has a 1080p HD camera and two-way audio.

The Eufy Video Doorbell is another great option with smart home integration. It works with Alexa and Google Assistant and has a 2K resolution camera with HDR. It also has advanced features such as human detection and facial recognition.

Affordable Video Doorbells

Video doorbells can range from a few hundred dollars to over a thousand dollars, depending on their features and specifications. However, there are also affordable options available that provide basic functionality without breaking the bank.

The TP-Link Kasa Smart Wi-Fi Doorbell is one of the best affordable options available in Australia. It has a 1080p HD camera with night vision and two-way audio. It also has customizable motion detection and works with Alexa and Google Assistant.

The Swann Wire-Free Video Doorbell is another affordable option. It has a 1080p HD camera with night vision and two-way audio. It also has a rechargeable battery, making it easy to install without any wiring.
