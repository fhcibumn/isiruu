Where to Watch John Wick 3 in the UK - Latest News in 2023

John Wick 3: Parabellum - The Third Installment in the Franchise

John Wick 3: Parabellum was released in the UK in May 2023 and it has been one of the most anticipated movies of the year. The movie has been praised for its action sequences, stunning visuals and the return of Keanu Reeves as the titular character. It is the third installment in the franchise and follows John Wick as he fights for survival after being declared excommunicado by the High Table.

If you are a fan of the franchise, you might be wondering where you can watch John Wick 3 in the UK. The good news is that the movie is now available on various streaming platforms and in cinemas across the country. In this article, we will take a look at some of the options available for you to watch John Wick 3 in the UK.

Watch John Wick 3 on Netflix UK

Netflix UK - The Leading Streaming Platform

Netflix UK is the leading streaming platform in the UK and it has been expanding its library with new content regularly. John Wick 3 is now available to stream on Netflix UK, so if you have a subscription, you can watch the movie at no extra cost.

The great thing about Netflix is that you can watch the movie on any device, including your TV, laptop, tablet or smartphone. If you haven't subscribed to Netflix yet, you can sign up for a free trial and watch John Wick 3 without paying a penny.

Watch John Wick 3 on Amazon Prime Video

Amazon Prime Video - An Alternative Streaming Platform

If you prefer Amazon Prime Video over Netflix, you can also watch John Wick 3 on this platform. You can either rent or buy the movie, depending on your preference. If you choose to rent, you will have 48 hours to watch the movie and you can watch it on any device that supports Amazon Prime Video.

Alternatively, if you buy the movie, you can watch it as many times as you want and it will be added to your library. Amazon Prime Video is also available on various devices, including your Smart TV, Fire Stick, gaming consoles, and mobile devices.

Watch John Wick 3 in Cinemas

Cinemas - The Traditional Way to Watch Movies

If you prefer to watch movies in cinemas, you can also watch John Wick 3 in various cinemas across the UK. The movie has been released in both standard and IMAX formats, so you can choose the one that suits you best.

Watching John Wick 3 in cinemas will give you a more immersive experience and you can enjoy the movie on a big screen with superior sound quality. It's also a great way to support your local cinema and the movie industry as a whole.
