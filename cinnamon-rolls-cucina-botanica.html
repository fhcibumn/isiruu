The Ingredients

Unlike traditional cinnamon rolls that are loaded with sugar, butter, and white flour, cinnamon rolls Cucina Botanica are made with wholesome, plant-based ingredients. The dough is made with a blend of whole wheat flour, spelt flour, and flaxseed meal, which gives it a nutty, earthy flavor and a hearty texture. The filling is a mixture of cinnamon, coconut sugar, and almond butter, which creates a sweet, gooey filling that's bursting with flavor. And to top it all off, the rolls are drizzled with a creamy, vegan icing made with cashews, coconut milk, and maple syrup.

The Health Benefits

Thanks to the all-natural, organic ingredients, cinnamon rolls Cucina Botanica are not only delicious, they're also incredibly healthy. Whole wheat flour and spelt flour are both high in fiber, which helps to regulate digestion and keep you feeling full. Flaxseed meal is rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which are essential for brain health and can help to reduce inflammation in the body. Coconut sugar is a low-glycemic sweetener, which means it won't cause spikes in blood sugar levels like traditional sugar does. And almond butter is a great source of healthy fats and protein, which can help to keep you energized throughout the day.

At Cucina Botanica, they believe that food should not only be delicious and healthy, but also sustainable and environmentally friendly.
