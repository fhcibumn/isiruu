Come Cucinare i Fiori di Zucchina

Fiori di zucchina, or zucchini flowers, are a delicate and delicious ingredient in Italian cuisine. If you've never tried cooking with them before, you're missing out on a treat! Here are some tips for how to cook fiori di zucchina in a variety of dishes.

Fried Fiori di Zucchina

One of the most popular ways to prepare fiori di zucchina is to fry them. To do this, you'll need to prepare a batter using flour, salt, and beer. Mix the ingredients together until the batter has a smooth consistency. Then, gently dip the flowers into the batter, making sure they are coated evenly. Heat oil in a frying pan and carefully add the flowers to the hot oil. Fry until the batter is golden brown and crispy. Serve hot with a sprinkle of salt.

Stuffed Fiori di Zucchina

Another way to cook fiori di zucchina is to stuff them with a delicious filling. One popular filling is ricotta cheese mixed with herbs and spices. First, gently clean the flowers, removing any excess stems or leaves. Then, carefully stuff each flower with the ricotta mixture. Place the stuffed flowers in a baking dish and bake in the oven until the filling is cooked through and the flowers are tender. Serve hot with a drizzle of olive oil.

Fiori di Zucchina Pizza

Why not try adding fiori di zucchina to your pizza? Spread tomato sauce over the pizza crust, then add shredded mozzarella cheese and sliced fiori di zucchina. Bake in the oven until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Sprinkle with fresh basil before serving.

Fiori di Zucchina Risotto

Fiori di zucchina also make a delicious addition to risotto. Begin by sautéing diced onion in a large saucepan until translucent. Add the risotto rice and stir to coat in the onion mixture. Gradually add hot vegetable stock, stirring continuously, until the rice is cooked through. Add shredded fiori di zucchina and grated Parmesan cheese. Serve hot with a garnish of fresh parsley.
