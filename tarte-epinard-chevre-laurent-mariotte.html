Chef Laurent Mariotte's Tarte Epinard Chevre Recipe

If you're a fan of French cuisine, you've probably heard of chef Laurent Mariotte. He's a well-known chef in France, and his recipes are loved by many. One of his most popular dishes is the tarte epinard chevre, which is a savory tart made with spinach and goat cheese.

The recipe itself is relatively simple, but the combination of flavors is what makes this dish so delicious. The tart is made with a crust of buttery pastry, which is then filled with a creamy mixture of spinach, goat cheese, and eggs. The tart is then baked until golden brown and served warm. It's a perfect dish for any occasion, whether it's a fancy dinner party or a simple lunch at home.
