Where to Watch Impact Wrestling in 2023

Impact Wrestling continues to be one of the most exciting and action-packed wrestling promotions in the world. Fans from all around the world tune in to watch their favorite wrestlers compete in high-octane matches. However, with so many different streaming services and cable networks, it can be difficult to know where to watch Impact Wrestling in 2023. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best options for watching Impact Wrestling in 2023.

Streaming Services

One of the most popular ways to watch Impact Wrestling in 2023 is through streaming services. There are several different options available, including the Impact Plus app, Hulu, and Amazon Prime Video. The Impact Plus app is by far the best choice for diehard fans of the promotion. For just $7.99 per month, you'll get access to live streams of all Impact Wrestling events, as well as a huge library of past matches and exclusive content. Hulu and Amazon Prime Video also offer Impact Wrestling content, but not as much as Impact Plus.

If you're looking for a more affordable option, you may want to consider signing up for a free trial of one of these streaming services to catch a few episodes of Impact Wrestling. You can then decide if you want to continue your subscription or not.

Cable Networks

If you're a traditionalist and prefer watching wrestling on cable TV, there are several options available. The main network that airs Impact Wrestling is AXS TV. This network is available through most cable providers and offers live broadcasts of Impact Wrestling events. However, if you don't have AXS TV, you may want to consider subscribing to a cable package that includes it.

Another option is to check your local listings for other networks that may air Impact Wrestling events. Some regional sports networks and independent stations may air Impact Wrestling in your area.

Pay-Per-View Events

If you're a fan of big, blockbuster wrestling events, you may want to consider tuning in to Impact Wrestling's pay-per-view events. These events are available through most cable providers and streaming services. The cost can vary depending on the event, but they usually range from $30-$50. Pay-per-view events are a great way to watch your favorite wrestlers compete in high-stakes matches and are definitely worth the investment if you're a diehard fan.

Live Events

If you're lucky enough to live near a city where Impact Wrestling is hosting a live event, you definitely don't want to miss out. Live events offer a unique experience that can't be replicated through streaming services or cable TV. You'll get to see your favorite wrestlers up close and personal, feel the energy of the crowd, and witness some truly amazing matches. Check the Impact Wrestling website for a full list of upcoming live events.

Conclusion

No matter how you prefer to watch your wrestling, there are several different options available for watching Impact Wrestling in 2023. From streaming services to cable networks to live events, there is something for every type of wrestling fan. So, grab some popcorn, settle in, and get ready to watch some of the most exciting wrestling matches around!
