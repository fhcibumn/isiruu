The World of Eau de Vie 4 Lettres

Eau de vie, literally translated to "water of life", is a type of clear, colorless fruit brandy that is distilled from fermented fruit juice. Eau de vie 4 lettres are typically made from fruits such as pear, apple, plum, and cherry, and are known for their intense, fruity flavors and aromas.

These spirits originated in Europe and have been produced for centuries, with each region having its own distinct style and flavor profile. Eau de vie 4 lettres are typically consumed as a digestif, or after-dinner drink, and are often served chilled or at room temperature. They are also commonly used in cocktails to add a unique twist to classic recipes.
