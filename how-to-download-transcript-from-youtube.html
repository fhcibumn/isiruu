How to Download Transcript from Youtube

Youtube is one of the most popular video sharing platforms in the world. It is used by millions of people to watch and share videos on various topics. However, sometimes you may want to download the transcript of a particular video for various reasons. In this article, we will guide you on how to download transcript from Youtube.

Step 1: Open Youtube Video

The first step is to open the Youtube video for which you want to download the transcript. You can do this by searching for the video using the Youtube search bar or by clicking on the video link if someone has shared it with you.

Step 2: Click on Three Dots

Once the video is open, click on the three dots located below the video player. This will open a pop-up menu with several options.

Step 3: Click on Open Transcript

From the pop-up menu, click on the "Open transcript" option. This will open a new tab with the transcript of the video.

Step 4: Download Transcript

To download the transcript, click on the three dots located on the top right corner of the transcript tab. From the drop-down menu, click on the "Download transcript" option. The transcript will be downloaded in plain text format.

Conclusion

Downloading transcript from Youtube is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps. By following the steps mentioned in this article, you can easily download the transcript of any Youtube video that you want. We hope this article was helpful to you.
