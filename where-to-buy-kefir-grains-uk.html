Where to Buy Kefir Grains UK - A Comprehensive Guide

A Delicious and Nutritious Drink

Kefir is a fermented milk drink that has been around for centuries. It is made by adding kefir grains to milk, which then ferment over a period of 24-48 hours. The result is a tangy, slightly fizzy drink that is packed full of probiotics, vitamins, and minerals. Kefir has been shown to improve gut health, boost immunity, and even aid weight loss.

What are Kefir Grains?

Before we dive into where to buy kefir grains in the UK, it's important to understand what they are. Kefir grains are small, gelatinous clumps made up of bacteria and yeast. Despite the name, they are not actually grains, but rather a symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY). When added to milk, they ferment the lactose into lactic acid, which gives kefir its distinctive tangy flavor.

Kefir grains can be used to make milk kefir, water kefir, and even coconut milk kefir. They are reusable, so once you have a batch of kefir, you can use the same grains to make more. Over time, the grains will grow and multiply, so you can share them with friends or use them to make larger batches of kefir.
