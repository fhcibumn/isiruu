How Long Does It Take To Cook Tom Yum?

Tom Yum is a popular Thai soup that is known for its sour and spicy taste. It is made with a variety of ingredients, including lemongrass, lime juice, chili peppers, and shrimp. One of the most common questions people ask when making Tom Yum is how long it takes to cook. The answer is that it depends on the recipe and the method of cooking.

Cooking Tom Yum on the Stove

If you are making Tom Yum on the stove, it usually takes around 15-20 minutes to cook. The first step is to simmer the broth with the lemongrass and kaffir lime leaves for about 10 minutes to infuse the flavors. Then, you can add the other ingredients, such as the shrimp, mushrooms, and chili peppers, and let them cook for another 5-10 minutes. Finally, you can add the lime juice and fish sauce to taste and serve hot.

Using an Instant Pot

If you are using an Instant Pot to make Tom Yum, the cooking time can be reduced to around 5-7 minutes. The first step is to sauté the lemongrass and garlic in the Instant Pot for a few minutes to release the flavors. Then, you can add the other ingredients, such as the shrimp, mushrooms, and chili peppers, and pressure cook for around 5 minutes. Finally, you can add the lime juice and fish sauce to taste and serve hot.

Variations of Tom Yum

There are also different variations of Tom Yum, such as Tom Yum Goong (shrimp), Tom Yum Gai (chicken), and Tom Yum Hed (mushroom). The cooking time may vary slightly depending on the type of protein you are using. For example, if you are using chicken instead of shrimp, you may need to cook it for a few minutes longer to ensure that it is fully cooked.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the cooking time for Tom Yum can vary depending on the recipe and the method of cooking. Whether you are making it on the stove or in an Instant Pot, it usually takes around 5-20 minutes to cook. If you are using different types of protein, the cooking time may vary slightly. Regardless of the method or recipe you choose, Tom Yum is a delicious and flavorful soup that is sure to impress your family and friends.
