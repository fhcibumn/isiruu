Millions of tourists visit this palace every year to admire its beauty and learn about its history.

Before you plan your visit to the Reggia di Caserta, it is essential to know the ticket prices. Best Time to Visit

If you want to avoid the crowds, it is best to visit the Reggia di Caserta during the off-season, which is from November to March. During this time, the palace and park are less crowded, and you can enjoy the beauty of the palace without the hustle and bustle of tourists. However, if you prefer to visit during the peak season, which is from April to October, it is best to book your tickets in advance to avoid long lines and waiting times.

How to Get There

The Reggia di Caserta is easily accessible by public transport. If you are coming from Naples, you can take the train from Napoli Centrale station to Caserta station, which takes approximately 30 minutes.
