When Does MVP Get Announced in NBA? - News Blog Article 2023

Catchy Opening

The NBA season is in full swing and fans around the world are eagerly waiting for the announcement of the Most Valuable Player (MVP) award. This prestigious award is given to the player who has made the most significant impact on their team and the league as a whole. But when exactly does the MVP get announced in NBA? Let's find out!

Regular Season MVP

The NBA regular season usually ends in April, and the MVP award is usually announced in late June after the NBA Finals. This gives the voters, who are sports journalists and broadcasters, enough time to analyze the performances of the players throughout the season and make an informed decision. The award ceremony is usually held during a special event, where the winner is presented with the coveted Maurice Podoloff Trophy.

Playoff MVP

In addition to the regular season MVP award, there is also a playoff MVP award, which is given to the player who has made the biggest impact during the NBA playoffs. This award is announced shortly after the NBA Finals, and the winner is also presented with a trophy. However, it's worth noting that the regular season MVP award is considered more prestigious, as it takes into account the player's performance throughout the entire season, not just the playoffs.

Voting Process

The MVP award is voted on by a panel of sports journalists and broadcasters from around the world, who are selected by the NBA. Each voter selects their top five players, and the player with the most votes is awarded the MVP trophy. The voting process is kept confidential until the award ceremony, which adds to the excitement and anticipation of the announcement.

Past Winners

Some of the most notable past winners of the MVP award include Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, and Stephen Curry. These players have not only made a significant impact on their respective teams but have also helped shape the NBA as we know it today. It will be interesting to see who the next MVP will be and how they will contribute to the league.
