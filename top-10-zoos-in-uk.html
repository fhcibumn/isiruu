Top 10 Zoos in UK

Are you looking for a fun day out with your family or friends? Why not visit one of the top 10 zoos in the UK? With a variety of animals and activities, you are sure to have a great time. Here are the top 10 zoos in the UK:

1. Chester Zoo

Chester Zoo is one of the most popular zoos in the UK, with over 21,000 animals from 500 different species. The zoo has a range of habitats, from the African savannah to the tropical rainforest. Visitors can also enjoy a range of activities, such as a boat ride through the islands of Southeast Asia or a trip on the monorail.

2. London Zoo

London Zoo is located in the heart of the city and is home to over 650 species of animals. Visitors can see a range of animals, from tigers to penguins, and enjoy a range of activities, such as a walk through the rainforest or a visit to the Butterfly Paradise exhibit.

3. Edinburgh Zoo

Edinburgh Zoo is home to over 1,000 animals from around the world. The zoo is famous for its penguin parade, where visitors can see the penguins waddle around the zoo. Visitors can also see a range of other animals, from lions to pandas.

4. Bristol Zoo

Bristol Zoo is located in Clifton and is home to over 400 species of animals. The zoo has a range of habitats, from the Monkey Jungle to the Gorilla Island. Visitors can also enjoy a range of activities, such as a trip on the zoo train or a visit to the Butterfly House.

5. Whipsnade Zoo

Whipsnade Zoo is located in Bedfordshire and is one of the largest zoos in the UK. The zoo is home to over 3,500 animals from around the world, including elephants, tigers, and giraffes. Visitors can also enjoy a range of activities, such as a ride on the Jumbo Express or a visit to the In with the Lemurs exhibit.
