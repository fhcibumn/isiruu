A Look at the Family of the Thai Prime Minister

Thai Prime Minister Prayut Chan-o-cha has been in power for several years now, and his family has been in the spotlight as well. One of the most talked-about topics is his son, who goes by the name of "Takhin."

Takhin has been the subject of much speculation and rumors, with many people wondering who he is and what he does. Some say he is involved in politics, while others claim he is a businessman. There have even been rumors that he is being groomed to take over as Thailand's next prime minister.

However, little is known about Takhin and his activities. He keeps a low profile and rarely makes public appearances. Nevertheless, his name continues to be a topic of interest among Thai people, and many are curious to learn more about him and his family.

Prayut's Wife and Children

Aside from Takhin, Prayut has a wife and two daughters. His wife's name is Naraporn Chan-o-cha, and she has been married to Prayut for over 30 years. The couple has two daughters together, both of whom are also involved in politics.

Pratarnporn Chan-o-cha, the younger daughter, is a member of Parliament and serves as the Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Cooperatives. Pintongta Shinawatra, the elder daughter, is married to Panthongtae Shinawatra, the son of former Thai Prime Minister Thaksin Shinawatra.

Despite their high-profile status, Prayut's family members keep a relatively low profile and tend to avoid the media spotlight as much as possible.

Controversies and Criticisms

Prayut's family has not been immune to controversies and criticisms. In 2021, Naraporn was accused of violating COVID-19 protocols by attending a gathering without wearing a mask. She later apologized for her actions.

Prayut himself has been criticized for his handling of the COVID-19 pandemic and for his government's crackdown on pro-democracy protesters. His family's wealth and connections have also been a topic of debate among the Thai public.

Despite these criticisms, Prayut and his family remain influential figures in Thai politics, and their actions and decisions continue to shape the country's future.

The Future of the Chan-o-cha Family

As Prayut's term as prime minister comes to an end, many are wondering what the future holds for the Chan-o-cha family. Will Takhin become more involved in politics? Will one of Prayut's daughters follow in his footsteps and become the next prime minister?

Only time will tell what lies ahead for the Chan-o-cha family, but one thing is certain: their influence on Thai politics is unlikely to wane anytime soon.
