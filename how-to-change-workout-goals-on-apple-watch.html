How to Change Workout Goals on Apple Watch

Have you been using your Apple Watch to track your workouts, but now you want to change your workout goals? Whether you want to increase your daily calorie burn or change the type of workout you're tracking, it's easy to update your goals on your Apple Watch. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to change your workout goals on your Apple Watch.

Step 1: Open the Activity App

The first step is to open the Activity app on your Apple Watch. You can do this by pressing the Digital Crown on the side of your watch and selecting the Activity app from your app list.

Step 2: Tap on "Change Goals"

Once you're in the Activity app, swipe up on your watch face until you see the "Change Goals" option. Tap on this option to access your current workout goals.

Step 3: Adjust Your Goals

From here, you can adjust your daily calorie burn goal, your exercise minutes goal, and your stand goal. Simply use the plus and minus buttons to increase or decrease your goals. You can also choose to change the type of workout you're tracking by tapping on "Workout Type" and selecting a different option.

Additional Tips:

- If you don't see the "Change Goals" option, swipe left or right on your watch face until you see the "Activity" option and tap on it.
- You can also adjust your workout goals through the Activity app on your iPhone by going to "My Watch"> "Activity"> "Goals."

Conclusion

Changing your workout goals on your Apple Watch is a simple process that can help you stay motivated and on track with your fitness journey. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can easily adjust your goals to fit your current needs and preferences. So why wait? Start exploring your options and reach your fitness goals with your Apple Watch today!
