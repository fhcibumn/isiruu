What to Season Brussel Sprouts With: A Guide for 2023

Brussel sprouts have been a staple in many households for years, but sometimes it can be difficult to know what seasonings to use to make them the star of the dish. In this guide, we will explore some of the best seasonings to use when cooking brussel sprouts, so you can enjoy them in all their glory.

First, let's discuss the classic pairing of brussel sprouts with bacon. Bacon adds a salty, smoky flavor that complements the natural bitterness of the sprouts. To make this dish, simply cook bacon in a pan until crispy, remove from the pan, and add halved brussel sprouts to the bacon grease. Cook until the sprouts are tender and slightly browned, and then crumble the bacon over the top. Delicious!

Next up, we have garlic and parmesan. This flavor combination is a little more sophisticated, but equally delicious. Simply roast the brussel sprouts in the oven with some minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Once they are cooked, toss them with freshly grated parmesan cheese for a cheesy, garlicky delight.

If you're looking for a more exotic flavor profile, try seasoning your brussel sprouts with curry powder. Curry powder is a blend of spices that includes turmeric, cumin, coriander, and more. Toss your sprouts in some curry powder, olive oil, and salt, and roast them in the oven until they are crispy and golden brown. This is a great way to add some variety to your vegetable game.

Finally, we have a sweet and savory option: maple glazed brussel sprouts. To make this dish, simply roast your sprouts in the oven with some olive oil, salt, and pepper. When they are almost done, brush them with a mixture of maple syrup and dijon mustard, and return them to the oven for a few more minutes. The result is a deliciously sweet and tangy side dish.

Conclusion

There are so many ways to season brussel sprouts, and these are just a few of our favorites. Whether you prefer classic bacon, sophisticated garlic and parmesan, exotic curry powder, or sweet maple glaze, there's a seasoning combination out there for everyone. So the next time you're looking for a tasty vegetable side dish, be sure to give brussel sprouts a try!
