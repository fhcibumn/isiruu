Over the Counter Weight Loss That Works - News Blog 2023

Are you tired of trying every diet and exercise plan out there, only to see minimal results? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle to lose weight and keep it off. However, there is hope. Over the counter weight loss supplements can provide an effective solution for those seeking a little extra help in their weight loss journey. Here are some of the best options currently available:

Green Tea Extract

Green tea extract is a popular ingredient in many weight loss supplements. It contains catechins, which are antioxidants that can help boost metabolism and burn fat. One study found that participants who took green tea extract for 12 weeks lost an average of 2.9 pounds more than those who didn't take the supplement. Plus, green tea extract is generally safe and well-tolerated.

Conjugated Linoleic Acid (CLA)

CLA is a type of fatty acid that is found in meat and dairy products. It has been shown to help reduce body fat, particularly in the abdominal area. Studies have also found that CLA can help improve lean body mass. However, it's important to note that CLA supplements can cause some side effects, such as digestive issues and insulin resistance.

Garcinia Cambogia

Garcinia cambogia is a tropical fruit that contains hydroxycitric acid (HCA), which has been shown to suppress appetite and inhibit fat production. One study found that participants who took garcinia cambogia supplements lost an average of 2 pounds more than those who took a placebo. However, more research is needed to determine the long-term effects of this supplement.

Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar has gained popularity in recent years for its potential health benefits, including weight loss. It works by increasing feelings of fullness and reducing appetite. Some studies have also found that apple cider vinegar can help lower blood sugar levels. However, it's important to dilute apple cider vinegar before consuming it, as it can be harmful to tooth enamel and the esophagus.

Overall, over the counter weight loss supplements can be a helpful addition to a healthy diet and exercise plan. However, it's important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new supplement regimen. Remember to also prioritize sustainable and healthy weight loss habits over quick fixes.
